#### De ce aveti nevoie

* Ultima versiune de la [Decrypt9WIP](https://github.com/d0k3/Decrypt9WIP/releases/latest/)
* Ultima versiune de la [safehax](https://github.com/TiniVi/safehax/releases/latest)
* Ultima versiune de la [fasthax](https://github.com/nedwill/fasthax/releases/latest)

#### Instructiuni

1. Creeati un folder numit `files9` pe card daca nu exista deja
2. Copiati `safehax.3dsx` in folderul`/3ds/` de pe card
3. Copiati `fasthax.3dsx` in folderul `/3ds/` de pe card
4. Copiati `Decrypt9WIP.bin` din arhiva Decrypt9WIP `.zip` pe card si redenumiti `Decrypt9WIP.bin` in `safehaxpayload.bin`
5. Reintroduceti cardul SD in 3DS
6. Intrati in lansatorul homebrew
7. Lansati fasthax
8. Odata ce s-a terminat, apasati (Start) ca sa iesiti inapoi in lansatorul homebrew
  + Asta poate lua cateva incercari
9. Lansati safehax
10. Daca exploatarea a avut loc cu succes, ar trebui sa fiti in Decrypt9 acum

Continuati la [2.1.0 ctrtransfer](2.1.0-ctrtransfer)
