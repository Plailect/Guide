所有的卡帶都有附上玩那遊戲所需要的系統更新，讓擁有比較舊的系統版本的玩家可以在離線的狀態下更新系統。
{: .notice--info}

{% capture notice-1 %}

請注意，卡帶更新僅會更新系統的核心組件，像是系統設定、HOME 主選單等。 Cart Updates will not deliver updates to Nintendo 3DS Sound and Network features, such as System Transfer, Internet Browser, StreetPass Mii Plaza, or eShop.
<br><br>
This means that using a Cart Update from a version containing an older Nintendo 3DS Sound version *(<7.0.0 for Old 3DSs in the EUR, JPN, KOR, and USA regions)* to one that introduced a newer Nintendo 3DS Sound version will break [Soundhax](homebrew-launcher-(soundhax))! 你將會需要透過[另一個方法](homebrew-launcher-(alternatives))才能使用 Homebrew Launcher。
{% endcapture %}

<div class="notice--warning">{{ notice-1 | markdownify }}</div>

意思也就是卡帶更新不會在你沒有瀏覽器的情況下給你安裝一個瀏覽器。 他**只會**更新實際的系統版本而已。*（例：2.1.0-0 -> 6.0.0-0）* 版本號碼的最後一個數字*(-0的部分)***不會**改變。
{: .notice--warning}

**Updating with a game cart containing the version 9.9.0 or above will remove your browser until a standard online update is performed!**
{: .notice--danger}

#### 必備項目

* A game cart that [contains the version](http://www.3dsdb.com/) you wish to update to

#### 操作說明

1. Boot your device into recovery mode by holding (L) + (R) + (A) + (UP) while powering on
1. If prompted, decline the update
1. Reboot
1. Put the game in your device if it is not already
1. 試圖啟動遊戲
1. 當提示時，選擇進行更新
