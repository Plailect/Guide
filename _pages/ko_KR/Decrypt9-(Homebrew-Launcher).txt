준비물

* 최신 버전의 [디크립트9WIP](https://github.com/d0k3/Decrypt9WIP/releases/latest/)
* 최신 버전의 [세이프핵스](https://github.com/TiniVi/safehax/releases/latest)
* 최신 버전의 [패스트핵스](https://github.com/nedwill/fasthax/releases/latest)

지침

1. SD card 최상위 경로에 'files9' 이라는 폴더가 존재하지 않을 경우 폴더를 만드십시오.
2. `safehax.3dsx` 파일을 SD card의 3ds 폴더에 복사하십시오.
3. `fasthax.3dsx` 파일을 SD card의 3ds 폴더에 복사하십시오.
4. Decrypt9WIP `.zip`파일에서 `Decrypt9WIP.bin`을 `safehaxpayload.bin`으로 수정해 SD card 최상위 경로에 복사하십시오.
5. Reinsert your SD card into your 3DS
6. Enter the homebrew launcher
7. Launch fasthax
8. Once it has completed, press (Start) to exit back to the homebrew launcher
  + This may take several tries
9. Launch safehax
10. If the exploit was successful, you will have booted into Decrypt9
