Velg den aktuelle siden for din versjon fra diagrammet nedenfor.
{: .notice--primary}

Note that the "from" and "to" fields are inclusive. Dette betyr at for eksempel, raden "fra 9.0.0 til 9.2.0" inkluderer 9.0.0, 9.1.0, og 9.2.0.
{: .notice--info}

Note that the last number of your system version (which is preceded by a dash) refers to your browser version. Currently, the browser version is irrelevant to the New 3DS in this guide.
{: .notice--info}

For all versions you can also [Cart Update](cart-update) to a higher version in the same column to follow its instructions.
{: .notice--info}

Your device version can be found at the bottom right of the top screen of the System Settings.
{: .notice--success}
