Aquest projecte és la culminació de centenars i centenars d'hores de treball constant.

Mantinc aquesta guia, gratuïta i m'asseguro que està sempre actualitzada. Because of this effort, I feel that it is fair for me to attempt to gain a meager income from my work (I receive next to no donations anymore).

This site costs you nothing to use, and all that I display are non-intrusive ads. If you still have a problem with this, feel free to block these ads.

If you do block ads, I would like to also remind you of the [Donations](donations) page, but once again that is optional too.
